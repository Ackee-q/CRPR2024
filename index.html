The logo of CD Projekt Red (CDPR) features a striking and stylized red bird, known as the "Raróg." This mythical creature, inspired by Slavic folklore, symbolizes the studio's Polish roots and creative spirit. The bird is depicted in a dynamic, angular design, giving it a modern and distinctive look. The logo is often accompanied by the company's name, "CD Projekt Red," in a clean and bold typeface. This combination of the mythical bird and contemporary typography represents the studio's blend of traditional influences and innovative game development.

The CD Projekt Red office in Warsaw is a modern and dynamic workspace that reflects the creative and innovative spirit of the studio. Located in a sleek, contemporary building, the office boasts state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant atmosphere. The interior design emphasizes open spaces, with collaborative areas that encourage teamwork and creativity among employees.
